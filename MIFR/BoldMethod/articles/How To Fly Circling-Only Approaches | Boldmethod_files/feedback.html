In the late 80's I was a Captain on a Beech 1900 for Business Express Airlines. I was doing the VOR runway one approach circle to runway 33. I leveled of at the MDA with runway one in sight and continues the approach. There was scattered clouds between our aircraft and the airport. I began to lose sight of runway one but picked up runway 33 and continued. When I began to lose sight of runway 33 runway one came back into view. This transition happened about two or three times until I made my landing on runway 33. I don't remeber why I did not do the approach to runway one. It may have been the wind or that runway one was too short and our Ops Specs did not allow a landing on that runway.
